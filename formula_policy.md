# Formula Policy

SciFigure treats equations as scientific content. Equations should remain editable whenever the backend supports it.

## Priority

1. MathType object.
2. PowerPoint Office Math object.
3. Clear equation placeholder and `equations.md` source file.

## Fallback

When native equation insertion is unreliable, place a visible equation placeholder in the slide and record the source in `equations.md`.

## Rules

- Keep equation labels and explanatory text editable.
- Do not use unreadable plain-text formulas.
- Do not hide important formulas inside generated images unless the user explicitly approves it.
